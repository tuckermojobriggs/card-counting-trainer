Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real-money gambling?
No. Card Counting Trainer is a training simulator for entertainment and educational purposes only. All chips are play money with no real-world value, and there is no way to deposit, win, or withdraw real money.

Where is my data stored?
Your practice statistics and settings are stored locally on your device and are never sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I reset my stats?
Open the Settings tab in the app to manage your saved data.
