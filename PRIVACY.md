# Privacy Policy

**Card Counting Trainer · Last updated June 8, 2026**

Card Counting Trainer ("the app") is an educational Blackjack training simulator. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains, in plain terms, how the app handles your information.

## Information We Collect

**We collect nothing.** The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal data.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Your practice statistics and app settings are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device, is never sent to us or any third party, and is removed if you delete the app.

## Network and Third Parties

The app works entirely offline. It makes no network connections, contains no advertising, includes no analytics or tracking, and shares no data with any third parties.

## Children's Privacy

The app is intended for entertainment and educational use and contains simulated gambling content. Because no personal information is collected from anyone, the app does not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a revised date above.
